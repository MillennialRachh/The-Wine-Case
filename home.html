The Wine Case

The Wine Case is a subsciption box offering the
finest selection of wine, derived from six regions,
delivered to your doorstep. For many centuries,
the taste of wine has been greatly appreciated,
thoroughly embedded into a number of cultures.
Our comprehensive wine and food pairing guide
has been carefully crafted to help you decide on
which flavours are best paired together.

How to order your case

Pack your case


Select 6 bottles of wine from
any of the regions. Each case
comes with a complimentary
food and wine pairing guide.

Pick a subscription


When your case is packed and
ready to go, select a subscription
model that quenches your thirst.

Choose a delivery method


We have a range of delivery
methods available, 7 days a
week. Delivered by our trusted
courier partners.

Enjoy on us


We will send you quarterly minature
samples of bottles of wine you have
not yet induldged in. Also, a sample
of new wine additions!
